## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Future redistribution starts after imported elapsed history
When the CIDIS 2026 import is applied, the system SHALL regenerate future assignments starting on `2026-07-10` while preserving imported elapsed history through `2026-07-09`.

The generated remainder of 2026 SHALL continue to prevent consecutive assignments and balance workload proportionally to availability.

#### Scenario: Import regenerates after current date
- **GIVEN** the CIDIS 2026 import has loaded elapsed history through `2026-07-09`
- **WHEN** future generation runs
- **THEN** automatic assignments before `2026-07-10` remain unchanged
- **AND** required coverage dates from `2026-07-10` onward are generated by the current algorithm

#### Scenario: Remainder of year remains balanced
- **GIVEN** all current managers and vacations from the CIDIS 2026 import are available to the generator
- **WHEN** the remainder of 2026 is generated
- **THEN** the resulting schedule has no same-manager consecutive days
- **AND** no active manager is assigned while on vacation or vacation buffer
